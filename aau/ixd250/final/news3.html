Fresh Air, Fresh Space: New Outdoor Area for Employees

We’ve expanded our facilities with a dedicated outdoor area giving employees a relaxing space to enjoy breaks, connect with colleagues, and recharge in the fresh air.

What’s New?

  • Bench seating for moments of solitude or quick chats
  • Open space to relax and clear your mind
  • Quiet area to unwind, recharge, or get some fresh air
  • Natural surroundings to connect with the outdoors

This space may be simple, but it’s designed to help you slow down, reset, and feel refreshed. A little dirt and open sky can go a long way in recharging your focus and creativity.

Step Outside and Breathe

We encourage you to make use of this new space. Whether it’s a brief break or a quick pause in your day, take a moment to enjoy the outdoors and get some fresh air.
