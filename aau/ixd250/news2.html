Prioritizing Safety: Fire Extinguishers Installed at Every Fuel Stand

At InvestOil, safety isn’t just a priority it’s a core value. In a continued effort to protect our clients, employees, and the environment, we’ve taken a significant step forward: every InvestOil fuel stand is now equipped with a dedicated fire hydrant.

Every fuel stand is now equipped with its own dedicated fire hydrant, providing faster emergency access and an extra layer of protection where it matters most.

What’s New?

  • A fire hydrant installed directly at every fueling point
  • Faster response time in case of emergencies
  • Improved safety for customers, staff, and surrounding areas
  • Smarter infrastructure that supports long term site resilience

We know that fueling stations come with their own unique risks. That’s why we’ve gone beyond the basics to build safety directly into our setup. Whether you're filling up or working on-site, we want you to feel confident that safety is always top priority.

Why It Matters

This upgrade helps ensure that emergency responders and trained team members have immediate access to water if a fire ever breaks out. No delays, no searching for the nearest connection—just quick, direct action to keep people safe and minimize damage.

At InvestOil, safety isn’t a feature it’s part of our foundation..

Come See the Difference

Whether you’re visiting a station or part of the team, we invite you to check out this small but powerful upgrade. It’s one of the many ways we’re investing in safer, smarter fueling spaces—for today and for the future.

As always, if you have questions or ideas about how we can keep improving, we’re all ears.
