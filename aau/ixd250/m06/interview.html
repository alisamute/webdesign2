Q : What is your all-time favorite candy, and why?

A : Chocolate candies, for sure. There’s something about the richness and smoothness that fruity candies just can’t match.

Q : What chocolate brands do you prefer?

A : Peanut M&M’s! They’re the perfect combo of chocolate, crunch, and a little saltiness. Still a go-to snack.

Q : Is there a candy from your childhood that you still love today?

A : Sweet, but with a little something extra—like caramel with sea salt or dark chocolate with chili.

Q : What is your favorite type of sweetner and why did you choose that one?

A : Honey! It’s naturally sweet, complex, and comforting, but you don’t see it much in mainstream candies.

Q : What is the most overrated flavor that everyone loves but you tend to not enjoy.

A : Black licorice. It’s always been a struggle to enjoy—too bitter and medicinal.

Q : What is your favorite or best candy you have tried?

A : Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups. The balance of chocolate and peanut butter is just unbeatable.
