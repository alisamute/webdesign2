Fueling Our Team: New Cafeteria Now Open

Our updated cafeteria space is now open offering a clean, comfortable environment where team members can enjoy meals, take breaks, and recharge during the workday.

We’re excited to welcome everyone into the refreshed space! Whether you’re swinging by for your morning coffee, catching up with coworkers over lunch, or just looking for a place to reset between meetings, the new cafeteria was designed to make your day better.

What’s New?

  • Modern, comfy seating for both solo moments and group hangouts
  • Improved lighting and natural tones to create a calm, energizing vibe
  • More food options and better kitchen appliances to keep up with your cravings
  • Stronger Wi-Fi and more outlets for when you want to plug in while you power up
  • Sustainable choices like energy-efficient lighting, reusable dishware, and recycling stations

We’ve put a lot of thought into how people actually use the space—so there are now designated zones for different kinds of breaks. Want to catch up on emails? Grab a quiet corner. Need a midday brainstorm? Tables are ready. Just want to eat in peace? Totally doable.

Come Check It Out!

We’d love for everyone to explore the new space, try out the seating, and enjoy the refreshed menu options. It’s more than just a place to eat.

It’s a place to pause, connect, and take a breath during the busy workday.

Let us know what you think, we're always looking for ways to make the workplace even better.
